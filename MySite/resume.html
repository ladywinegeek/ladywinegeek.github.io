I'm a Chicago-based User Experience Designer and am especially passionate about storytelling. The better we understand someone's story, the better we're able to understand their point-of-view, their problem, and how best to help them. This, combined with creativity, curiosity and sense of adventure, I hope to collaborate with others who also want to improve the communication and interactions between people and the services they use.

Projects


Explore Chicago

For this independent project, I created a mobile app to help newcomers to Chicago find events outside of the heavily-trafficked downtown area.


TrueSpirit

An independent project for an online school uniform retailer where I designed a website that enabled users to find and purchase the items they needed as well as return, review, and repurchase those items with as little hassle as possible.


Moxy Hotel

A collaborative project where my group and I revamped the checkin/checkout process for the hotel as well as created features and content that would inform users about the hotel space and help them connect with other guests.
